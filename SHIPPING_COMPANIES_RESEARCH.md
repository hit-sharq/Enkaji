## Next Steps for Implementation

1. **Create Shipping Zones Database**
   - Define zones (Nairobi, Kenya, East Africa, International)
   - Map countries to zones

2. **Weight Calculation Logic**
   - Calculate volumetric weight vs actual weight
   - Use the higher of the two

3. **Provider Selection**
   - Auto-select based on destination and weight
   - Allow user choice at checkout

4. **Delivery Options**
   - Standard (5-7 days)
   - Express (2-3 days)
   - Same-day (Nairobi only)

5. **Insurance**
   - Optional package insurance (1-3% of value)
   - Mandatory for high-value items

6. **Tracking**
   - Generate tracking numbers
   - Email/SMS notifications
   - Tracking page on website
